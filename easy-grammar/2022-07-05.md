- Simple past tense
  - actions that started and ended in the past
  - at a specific point in time
  - can use for repeated actions in the past (+ frequency indicator)

- Regular Verbs
  - Talk -> Talked
  - Start -> Started
  - Enjoy -> Enjoyed

- Irregular Verbs
  - Eat -> Ate
  - Speak -> Spoke
  - Make -> Made
  - Go -> Went
  - Forget -> Forgot
  - Say -> Said
  - Sleep -> Slept
